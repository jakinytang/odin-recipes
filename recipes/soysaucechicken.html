Soy Sauce Chicken Recipe

Soy Sauce Chicken

Description

This super simple recipe is for Cantonese style soy sauce chicken. The sweet and savory combination of flavors along with the juicy texture of chicken makes for a wonderful comfort food

Ingredients

for 4 servings

  • 4 chicken quarters
  • 1 bottle (410 ml) of Lee Kum Kee sweet soy sauce
  • 4 thin slices of ginger
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp of cooking wine
  1. Pour entire bottle of sweet soy sauce into large pot and add 1:1 ratio of water
  2. Bring large pot to rolling boil and then lower heat to simmer
  3. Trim as much fat off chicken quarters with skin remaining on
  4. Add ginger, garlic, and cooking wine to the pot
  5. Add chicken quarters to the pot
  6. Loosely cover and simmer for 10 minutes (make sure cover is loose or the contents will boil over)
  7. Remove chicken from pot and let cool for 5 minutes
  8. Use cleaver to chop chicken into pieces
  9. Serve with spoonfuls of soy sauce mixture drizzled over green vegetables (I prefer Choy Sum)
