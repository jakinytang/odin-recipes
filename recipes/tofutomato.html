Tofu Egg Tomato Recipe

Description

This Tomato Egg Tofu Stir Fry is sweet and sour and savory to combine simple ingredients into complex flavors and pairs perfectly with a warm bowl of jasmine rice

Ingredients

  • 1 lb medium firm tofu (chopped into 2"x2"x0.5" slices)
  • 1 large red tomato (chopped into edible chunks)
  • 2 eggs (well beaten)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp of fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp of brown sugar
  • 1 stalk of green onions (sliced lenthwise to 2")
  1. Add oil to a pan and heat on medium/high
  2. Pan fry tofu for 3 minutes on each side until golden brown, adding salt and pepper to taste (Remove and set aside in large bowl)
  3. Cook eggs scrambled in pan adding salt and pepper to taste (Remove and add to same bowl
  4. Cook tomatoes on high heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. As tomatoes breakdown, add oyster sauce, fish sauce and brown sugar, and 3 tbsp of water
  6. Once the tomatoes boil again add tofu and egg and gently stir pan to mix all ingredients together
  7. Add green onions to the mixture and remove from heat
  8. Serve hot and fresh with a bowl of jasmin rice and other dishes
